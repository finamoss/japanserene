Our Mission

Japan Serene restaurant is a globally influenced Japanese-inspired restaurant nestled in the heart of Chicago. Executive Chef Timothy Runes brings with him years of experience and leads the kitchen team with innovation and superb technique. Japan Serene offers an array of options with many small and large plates, speciality craft cocktails and a full sushi bar stacked with sashimi and nigiri options flown fresh from Japan. Sourcing the freshest ingredients both locally and internationally, guests dining with us can experience our commitment to quality.

Japan Serene distinguishes itself by emphasizing on the social experience and taking a vibe-driven approach to fine dining. The dining room, sushi lounge and cocktail bar evokes a fun sense of style matched by the chic ambiance and decor.Whether you are joining us for dinner, cocktails, a private party or any special occasion, Japan Serene opens its doors to you and promises to make the experience special and memorable. The team eagerly awaits to show you world-class hospitality.
